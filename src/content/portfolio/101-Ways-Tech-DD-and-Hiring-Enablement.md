## Client
101 Ways (Technology Consultancy Firm)

## Engagement Period
Aug 2019 – Present

## Overview
Protize partners with 101 Ways to strengthen consulting outcomes through structured technology due diligence, technical assessments, and senior contractor hiring support.  
The engagement helps de-risk delivery for new and existing clients while improving team quality and execution capacity.

## Business Context
As a consultancy serving enterprise clients, 101 Ways requires reliable technical evaluations and strong hiring pipelines to maintain delivery quality across varied projects and domains.

## Challenges
- Need for consistent due diligence standards across engagements
- Requirement to assess technical risk before/during project delivery
- Demand for high-quality contractor hiring at senior levels
- Need to accelerate recruitment decisions without compromising quality

## Protize Scope
- Project-level technology due diligence for new and existing client work
- Technical assessments across architecture, delivery readiness, and engineering quality
- Recruitment supervision and interview process design
- Screening, interviewing, and hiring recommendations for senior contractors

## What We Delivered
- Structured due diligence and assessment support for consulting engagements
- Practical risk identification and quality-focused technical recommendations
- End-to-end interview support for contractor hiring
- Senior talent evaluation from Principal to Senior Developer levels
- Ongoing advisory collaboration with delivery and hiring stakeholders

## Outcomes
- Improved confidence in project onboarding and delivery readiness
- Stronger technical hiring quality across consultancy client programs
- Faster, more reliable contractor selection for senior engineering roles
- Hiring and assessment support delivered across clients including:
  - Marks & Spencer
  - Tesco
  - Hargreaves Lansdown

## Team & Delivery
Protize works as a strategic partner to 101 Ways, combining technical evaluation discipline with practical hiring execution to support enterprise consulting outcomes.
